Description

Guildle is a hand-drawn idle incremental RPG where you recruit heroes, build your guild, and grow stronger through steady progression.

Set in a cozy fantasy world with a light JRPG feel, Guildle puts the player in the role of the Guild Master. Heroes head out on adventures, battle automatically, and help gather the resources needed to grow the guild further.

Players can recruit heroes, arrange party members, unlock facilities, buy and upgrade weapons, and develop a stronger team over time. Guildle is designed as a blend of idle progression, party-building, and hand-crafted fantasy presentation.

Features

  • Hand-drawn art across heroes, enemies, and environments
  • Idle incremental progression built around steady growth
  • Up to 12 unique heroes to unlock
  • Party building with simple RPG decision making
  • Guild facilities that support and expand the adventure
  • Weapon purchasing and upgrades
  • Dragon pets that assist progression through auto-clicking
  • Full auto hero skills that activate during battle
