Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

HAMMERHEAD Studio respects your privacy and aims to keep our data practices simple and understandable.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information related to the HAMMERHEAD Studio website and Guildle. If we release another game with materially different data practices, we may publish a separate or supplemental privacy notice for that product.

1. Who We Are

HAMMERHEAD Studio is the studio responsible for the website and Guildle.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can contact us at:

HAMMERHEAD Studio

Email: hammerhead8392@gmail.com

2. What This Policy Covers

This Policy applies to:

  • the HAMMERHEAD Studio website;
  • Guildle when distributed through Steam; and
  • communications you send directly to us.

This Policy does not replace the privacy policies of third-party platforms and services such as Steam, YouTube, or Discord. Those services may also collect and process information under their own terms and policies.

3. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

If you contact us by email, we may receive:

  • your name or display name;
  • your email address; and
  • the contents of your message and any attachments you send.

B. Website and Embedded Content

Our website does not use its own analytics tools beyond what is reasonably necessary to operate the site.

However, some pages may include embedded third-party content such as YouTube videos or Discord widgets. When you load or interact with those features, those third parties may collect information directly from your browser or device, such as technical information, cookie data, and interaction data, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

C. Steam and Guildle Data

When you play Guildle on Steam, we may receive or access certain information through Steamworks features that we use for the operation and improvement of the game.

Depending on the features enabled within Guildle, this may include:

  • gameplay related statistics;
  • achievement and progression information;
  • save data stored through Steam Cloud;
  • store and marketing reporting data made available through Steamworks;
  • crash or error information, if platform-supported reporting is enabled.

Where applicable, some of this information may be associated with your Steam account by Valve.

D. Aggregate Steam Reporting

We may also view aggregate or summary reporting provided through Steamworks, such as traffic, store performance, wishlist, conversion, and campaign reporting. We use this information to understand how players find Guildle and how the game performs on Steam.

4. How We Use Information

We use information covered by this Policy to:

  • operate Guildle and website related services;
  • enable game features that rely on Steamworks;
  • maintain save functionality, achievements, stats, and leaderboards;
  • diagnose bugs, crashes, and technical issues;
  • improve game stability, balance, usability, and overall player experience;
  • understand store performance and audience interest on Steam;
  • respond to support requests, business inquiries, and press inquiries;
  • protect our games, website, players, and business from abuse or misuse; and
  • comply with legal obligations.

5. What We Do Not Do

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not operate a separate HAMMERHEAD account system for Guildle at this time.

We do not use our own standalone website analytics platform at this time beyond basic website operation and third-party embedded content.

6. Sources of Information

We collect information:

  • directly from you, when you contact us;
  • automatically through your use of our website or third-party embedded content;
  • from Valve and Steamworks, when you use Guildle through Steam and the relevant Steamworks features are enabled.

7. Sharing of Information

We may share or make information available in the following situations:

A. Platform and Service Providers

We rely on third-party providers to operate parts of our website and game-related services. This may include:

  • Valve / Steam;
  • website hosting providers;
  • YouTube, for embedded video content; and
  • Discord, for embedded community widgets or links.

B. Legal or Safety Reasons

We may disclose information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to:

  • comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or government request;
  • enforce our terms or protect our rights;
  • prevent fraud, abuse, security issues, or technical harm; or
  • protect the safety of users, players, staff, or the public.

C. Business Transfers

If HAMMERHEAD Studio is involved in a merger, acquisition, asset sale, reorganization, or similar transaction, information covered by this Policy may be transferred as part of that process, subject to applicable law.

8. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable under privacy law, we generally process information on one or more of the following bases:

  • to provide the website or game features you use;
  • for our legitimate interests in operating, improving, securing, and supporting our games and website;
  • to comply with legal obligations; and
  • where required, based on your consent.

9. Data Retention

We try to keep data only for as long as it is reasonably needed for the purposes described in this Policy.

In general:

  • contact emails and related correspondence may be kept for up to **24 months** after the last relevant interaction, unless a longer period is needed for legal, business, or support reasons;
  • locally retained diagnostic data, exported reports, or similar support records may be kept for up to **12 months**, unless a longer period is reasonably needed for security, fraud prevention, bug fixing, dispute resolution, or legal compliance;
  • data stored and retained by Steam or other third-party platforms is subject to those platforms’ own retention practices and policies.

We may retain aggregated, anonymized, or non-identifiable information for a longer period for analysis, planning, and recordkeeping.

10. International Processing

HAMMERHEAD Studio is based in Japan, and the third-party services we use may process data in other countries.

By using our website or Guildle, you understand that information may be processed in jurisdictions outside your place of residence, subject to applicable law and the practices of the relevant service providers.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our website and Guildle are not directed to children under 13, or under the minimum age required by local law to provide personal information online without parental consent.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us in a way that violates applicable law, please contact us and we will review the request.
